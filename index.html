You can leave caption or credit fields blank if you do not need to include that information. Only include "yes" in the VERTICAL field if the image is a vertical image. Otherwise you can leave this field blank.

Once finished, select all (CTRL+A on a PC or Command+A on Mac) and copy (CTRL+C or Command+C) the spreadsheet contents and paste data in the field below.
